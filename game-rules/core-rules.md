## 3. Luck

Luck is the wildcard stat that affects crits, loot, and gives a once-per-session safety net.

| Mechanic | How Luck Applies |
|----------|-----------------|
| **Critical Range** | Nat 20 always crits. LCK 5+ also crits on 19. LCK 8+ crits on 18. LCK 12+ crits on 17. |
| **Loot Bonus** | Add LCK bonus to loot roll (d20 + LCK bonus on the loot table) |
| **Lucky Save** | Once per session, reroll any single d20 (free, no resource cost) |
| **Lucky Find** | GM can call for a LCK check to find hidden items, passages, or bonus treasure |

## 5. Combat

**Initiative:** d20 + Agility bonus. Highest first.
**Each Turn:** 1 Move (6 studs) + 1 Action + Resource Regen (Energy/Mana +20, or Rage from sources)

| Action | How |
|--------|-----|
| Melee Attack | d20 + STR bonus vs. enemy STR + 8. Hit = STR damage |
| Ranged Attack | d20 + AGI bonus vs. enemy AGI + 8. Hit = weapon damage |
| Cast Ability | d20 + MNA bonus vs. target. Uses Energy/Rage/Mana |
| Use Item | Potion, bomb, gadget. No roll |
| Defend | Skip attack. Enemies need +4 to hit you |
| Help Friend | Ally gets +3 on next roll |

**Knocked Out** at 0 HP (not dead). Ally revives with action (1 HP) or potion.
