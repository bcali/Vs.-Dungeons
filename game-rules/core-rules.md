## 1. Stats

Every hero has 4 stats. All start at **3**. At Level 1, add **+2** to any stats (can split, e.g. +1 STR / +1 SPD).

| Stat | What It Does |
|------|-------------|
| **STRENGTH** | Melee damage, lifting, breaking, carrying |
| **SPEED** | Turn order, dodging, ranged accuracy, movement |
| **TOUGHNESS** | Hit Points (TGH Ã— 3 + 5), resist poison/traps |
| **SMARTS** | Spells, spotting secrets, puzzles, crafting |

**Stat Bonus** = Stat âˆ’ 3 (Stat 3 = +0, Stat 5 = +2, Stat 10 = +7)
**Stat Cap** = 15

> **Example:** A Level 1 Knight puts both +2 into STR. STR becomes 5 (+2 bonus), everything else stays at 3 (+0). Or they split: +1 STR (4), +1 TGH (4) â€” both at +1 bonus.

## 3. Combat

**Initiative:** d20 + Speed bonus. Highest first.
**Each Turn:** 1 Move (6 studs) + 1 Action

| Action | How |
|--------|-----|
| Melee Attack | d20 + STR bonus vs. enemy TGH + 8. Hit = STR damage |
| Ranged Attack | d20 + SPD bonus vs. enemy SPD + 8. Hit = weapon damage |
| Cast Spell | d20 + SMT bonus vs. target. Uses spell slot |
| Use Skill Tree Ability | See ability description. Free (cooldown-based) |
| Use Item | Potion, bomb, gadget. No roll |
| Defend | Skip attack. Enemies need +4 to hit you |
| Help Friend | Ally gets +3 on next roll |

**Knocked Out** at 0 HP (not dead). Ally revives with action (1 HP) or potion.

### Hero Surge âš¡

**Once per battle**, any hero may **reroll any single die roll** (their own attack, a defense check, even a loot roll). This is a safety net against terrible luck streaks. Use it or lose it â€” doesn't carry between battles.

> *Lego tip: Give each player a special "Surge token" (a unique 1Ã—1 tile). They flip it face-down when used, flip it back at the start of the next battle.*

### Minions ðŸ’€

Some monsters are **Minions** â€” weak enemies with **1 HP**. Any hit kills a minion, no matter how much damage it would deal. Minions still deal their listed damage if they hit, but they exist to make heroes feel powerful.

Rules for Minions:
- Mark any monster as a minion by writing **(Minion)** after its name
- Any successful hit kills a minion â€” even 1 damage
- Minions still deal their normal listed damage on a hit
- The GM should include 2â€“3 minions in the first combat of every session
- Great for: Goblin Scouts, Giant Rats, Skeletons, any "pack" encounter

> *Example: "You face 3 Goblin Scout Minions and 1 Goblin Captain." The scouts die in one hit each, letting heroes feel awesome. The captain is a real fight.*

### Heroic Start ðŸŒŸ

**First session only:** All heroes roll attacks with **advantage** (roll 2d20, keep the higher) until they win their first combat encounter. After that first victory, normal rules apply.

This guarantees the first fight of the campaign feels heroic. By the time advantage drops off, the kids have confidence and momentum.

## 8. Skill Trees

Every profession has a **skill tree** â€” a branching web of abilities and bonuses unlocked with skill points. Think Diablo or World of Warcraft talents.

### How Skill Points Work

- Heroes earn **1 skill point per level** starting at Level 2 (2 at Level 20)
- At Level 1, every hero gets their **Starter Ability** auto-unlocked for free
- Points are spent on skills within your profession's tree
- Each branch has **tiers** â€” spend enough points in a branch to unlock the next tier
- Skills can have **ranks** (invest multiple points for stronger effects)

### Skill Types

| Type | Icon | How It Works |
|------|------|-------------|
| **Active** | âš”ï¸ | Goes in your Action Bar. Use as an action in combat. Free (cooldown-based). |
| **Passive** | ðŸ›¡ï¸ | Always on. No action needed. Permanent bonuses. |

### The Action Bar (5 Slots)

Heroes have **5 action bar slots** for active abilities.

- Only **active skills** (âš”ï¸) and **spellbook spells** go in slots
- **Passive skills** are always on â€” no slot needed
- Swap abilities freely outside of combat
- Normal melee/ranged attacks are always available (no slot needed)

### Skill Trees + Spellbook

The skill tree **extends** the spellbook system â€” it doesn't replace it.

| System | What It Provides | Cost to Use |
|--------|-----------------|-------------|
| **Skill Tree** | Talent tree skills + passives | **Free** (with cooldowns on strong abilities) |
| **Spellbook** | Profession spells (Battle Cries, Techniques, etc.) | **Spell Slots** |
| **Normal Attack** | Basic melee/ranged | **Free** (always available) |

This means tree abilities are your "always available" toolkit, while spellbook spells are limited-use power moves.

### Respec

**Free respec, anytime.** Kids can reset their entire tree and redistribute all points.

- No gold cost, no penalty
- Can respec between encounters, sessions, or during a rest
- Encourages experimentation
